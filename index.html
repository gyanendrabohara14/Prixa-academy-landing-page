4-Week Curriculum

24 Hours of Intensive Learning (6 Hours/Week)

Week 1

Branding & Digital Marketing Basics

  • Introduction to Branding (3 hrs): Core concepts of brand identity, values, and mission. Strategies for creating and managing brand identity.
  • Intro to Digital Marketing (3 hrs): Understanding the landscape and benefits.
  • Activity: Analyze a brand’s digital presence.

Week 2

Website, SEM & Social Media

  • Website Optimization (2 hrs): UX design, SEO basics, and CRO.
  • SEM & PPC (2 hrs): Google Ads fundamentals and keyword research.
  • Social Media (2 hrs): Platform strategy and content creation.
  • Activity: Mock PPC campaign & Audit a website.

Week 3

Content, Email & Mobile Marketing

  • Content Marketing (2 hrs): Strategy, creation (blogs, videos).
  • Email Marketing (2 hrs): List building, automation, and planning.
  • Mobile Marketing (2 hrs): App strategies and location-based tactics.
  • Activity: Create a content plan & Design email campaign.

Week 4

Analytics, Trends & Strategy

  • Analytics (2 hrs): Google Analytics, KPIs, and A/B testing.
  • Emerging Trends (2 hrs): Voice search, AI, and chatbots.
  • Strategy Development (2 hrs): Budgeting and integration.
  • Final Project: Create and present a complete digital marketing strategy.

Learning Outcomes

  • ✅ Master branding and digital marketing essentials.
  • ✅ Apply skills in website optimization, SEM, social media, and content marketing.
  • ✅ Develop effective email, mobile, and data-driven strategies.
  • ✅ Create a cohesive digital marketing plan for your own business or client.
